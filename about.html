About Sweet Fire Caribbean Grill Restaurant & Catering Services

How We Started

Jamaican-run restaurant, with a home grown chef... straight from Kingston. So you know the food is authentic, Jamaican “yard-style” cuisine. Marinated in our imported Jamaican spices and cooked to perfection— juicy jerk chicken, Jamaican oxtails, and delicious plantains are bursting with bold Caribbean flavor! But we’re not here to convince you... we’ll let our food speak for itself.
